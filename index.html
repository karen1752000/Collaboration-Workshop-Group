THE DISASTER ARTIST

The Disaster Artist is the real life story of writer/director Tommy Wiseau, the man behind what is often referred to as "The Citizen Kane of Bad Movies," The Room, is brought to life, chronicling the odd film's troubled development and eventual cult success. The cast of The Disaster Artist includes James Franco, Bryan Cranston, Seth Rogan, Sharon Stone, Dave Franco, and Zac Efron.

The Disaster Artist has received critical acclaim and has earned strong reviews from Rotten Tomatoes.

The Disaster Artist has made over 24 MILLION dollars worldwide, and only cost 10 MILLION to make.

COMPETITION FOR BEST PICTURE

The Disaster Artist has lots of competition for the best picture award at the 2018 Oscars from other movies like Dunkirk and The Shape Of Water. In total there is expected to be more than a dozen films nominated for the best picture award.
